For the fig-swirl; melt butter over medium heat in a sauce pan. Add brown sugar and stir to dissolve. Halve all of the figs and toss in the sauce pan with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until you have a chunky-jammy mixture. Add salt with one or two stirs, set aside and let cool completely.
